About Us

Hi guys! My name is Court. I grew up riding bikes with my family and friends in Colorado. That’s me with the pinwheel in the child seat at the back of the tandem!

Like many young people, bicycles brought me freedome and connection. Over the years, I rode my bike to visit friends, discover new places, and commute to school and work. As I got older, my bicycle helped me stay in shape and unwind after work ;)

I discovered electric bikes while living in Texas, because there were some big hils and hot hot days! My first ebike seemed a little heavy and expensive, and I actually regretted not spending more for a nicer one because I was riding it so much!

I launched ElectricBikeReview.com to share what I had learned and make it easier for people to find the right bike for their budget and lifestyle. The most common question I get is “what’s the best electric bike” but that answer really depends on the activity and budget. Each of us is different, and that’s why I cover and list so many different products here!

A basic affordable ebike could be great for a high schooler or college commuter where theft and damage is more likely, and the bike has to stay outside most of the time. A less comfortable but compact and light weight folding model could be perfect for that cramped RV or city apartment! Perhaps a relaxed cruiser with just one gear and no lights, racks, or fenders is all you really need for that happy neighborhood ride. It really depends on you... but I hope this site helps.

EBR remains founder owned and operated. I try to keep the advertising minimal and complimentary, we respect your privacy. I care about you, and this website is one way I am trying to make the world a little brighter... bicycles bring us together :)
