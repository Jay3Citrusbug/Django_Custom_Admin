EBR Advertising Services

Whether you’re a brand that designs and sells electric bikes, a local brick and mortar shop, or an online seller, we have targeted marketing services that will help you drive sales. Our services are measurable, and cost effective. Most importantly, they are authentic and complimentary for our visitors.

Ebike Brands

  • Our base service is a package deal including an official listing that directs visitors to your official website to make a purchase, combined with one promoted bike that appears at the top of your brand listing page and the “Related Ebikes” widget. If a competitor is not enrolled in this program, you can promote your bike on their pages too.
  • Our first high-end services allow you to promoted a bike in the main categories of the site, which also appear in the “Related Ebikes” widget for every review that is included in the category.
  • Our second high-end service allows you to support your dealer network with bulk pricing to add all shops on the local listing and map widgets. All services include analytics and monthly reporting, so you can see how promoted bikes are performing, and which which regions and shops are getting the most engagement. Contact us for pricing and more details.

Physical Shops:
